Shopware and Unleashed Technologies Announce eCommerce Partnership

By Unleashed on Oct 11, 2023

NEW YORK (October 11, 2023)—Global open-source ecommerce platform Shopware is pleased to announce a new partnership with Unleashed Technologies, a leading provider of enterprise and ecommerce websites, as well as custom software development, digital strategy and data structuring services for the Generative AI era. 

The partnership represents a natural fit as both companies are on a mission to deliver client-centric solutions, enabling merchants to leverage emerging technologies that support their ecommerce goals and simultaneously giving them access to pricing models that support their revenue goals. Traditionally, merchants have had access to either legacy enterprise solutions that are too expensive or cost-effective solutions that don’t offer the functionality they need to succeed. 

“This new partnership with Unleashed not only addresses a legacy issue for small and midsize businesses (SMBs) and entrepreneurs, but also signifies our continued commitment to ensuring that this community has access to leading edge technology which will ensure they can serve their customers well, while also growing their businesses,” said Justin Williams, Agency Partnerships, North America at Shopware. “The fact that Unleashed is determined to provide future-proof solutions for their customers is a bonus, aligning with our desire to create game changing ecommerce experiences. I have no doubt that by working together, we will continue to drive our industry forward.”

Merchants, manufacturers and distributors can now enjoy a wide range of ecommerce features that seamlessly integrate with their existing IT systems and websites, thanks to Shopware's API-first approach. Bolstered by the expertise and evolving services offered by Unleashed, they are now uniquely positioned to ensure merchants are set for success in this rapidly changing world. 

“We proudly go out of our way to ensure that we offer our clients the best solutions possible, which is why we’re always seeking to add partnerships that we think can make a measurable impact for both our current and future customers,” said Muhammad Hutasuhut, CEO at Unleashed Technologies. “Shopware’s technology enables us to offer industry-leading B2B and B2C eCommerce solutions that allow for the flexibility and customization discerning companies crave. To say we’re excited would be an understatement, as we believe this partnership will be a significant asset to clients at Unleashed.”

### 

About Shopware 

Shopware, founded in Schöppingen in 2000, offers a holistic omnichannel digital commerce platform for sophisticated mid-market and lower enterprise merchants, with high flexibility and various options for B2C, D2C, B2B, as well as service-based use cases. Shopware is well-positioned and one of the market leaders in its segment, especially in the DACH region, with a fast-growing business in North America.

The solution is headless, API-first, and uses an open-source architecture, allowing merchants to individually customize and scale the product offering, creating a strong customer experience. Furthermore, Shopware relies on a global network of 1,600 sales, technology and solution partners, securing a stable ecosystem. Shopware merchants currently process a combined GMV of more than $20 billion USD across the global Shopware platform.

About Unleashed Technologies

Unleashed Technologies provides enterprise and e-commerce websites, custom software development, digital strategy, and data structuring services for the Generative AI era. Since 2007, Unleashed has been committed to solving the most pressing business challenges using modern technology and custom development expertise.

Our Mission is to deliver client-centric, digital solutions by leveraging our expertise in open-source technologies and cross-platform integrations.

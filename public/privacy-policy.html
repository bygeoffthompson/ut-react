Privacy Policy

Unleashed is committed to protecting your privacy and developing technology that gives you a powerful and safe online experiences. This Statement of Privacy applies to the Unleashed website and governs data collection and usage. By using the Unleashed website or services, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

This privacy policy was last updated on December 12, 2018. Unleashed may modify the provisions of this privacy policy from time to time. The new provisions will be effective as soon as they are posted. Please check this page periodically to ensure that you are aware of any change.

Collection of your Personal Information

Unleashed collects personally identifiable information, such as your e-mail address, name, address or telephone number. Unleashed also collects anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you, such as your ZIP code, age, gender, preferences, and interests.

There is also information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically collected by Unleashed. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring Website addresses. This information is used by Unleashed for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of the Unleashed website.

Please keep in mind that if you directly disclose personally identifiable information or personally sensitive data through the Unleashed blog, this information may be collected and used by others .

Unleashed encourages you to review the privacy statements of websites you choose to link to from Unleashed so that you can understand how those websites collect, use and share your information. Unleashed is not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on websites outside of the Unleashed and Unleashed family of websites.

Use of your Personal Information

Unleashed collects and uses your personal information to operate the Unleashed website and deliver the services you have requested. Unleashed also uses your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from Unleashed and its affiliates. Unleashed may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.

Unleashed does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. Unleashed may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external business partners about a particular offering that may be of interest to you. In those cases, your unique personally identifiable information (e-mail, name, address, telephone number) is not transferred to the third party. In addition, Unleashed may share data with trusted partners to help us perform statistical analysis, send you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to Unleashed, and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

Unleashed does not use or disclose sensitive personal information without your explicit consent.

Unleashed keeps track of the websites and pages our customers visit within Unleashed, in order to determine what Unleashed services are the most popular. This data can be used to deliver customized content and advertising within Unleashed to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area.

Unleashed websites will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on Unleashed or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of Unleashed; and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of Unleashed, or the public.

Use of Cookies

The Unleashed website uses "cookies" to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.

One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the Web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you personalize Unleashed pages, or register with Unleashed site or services, a cookie helps Unleashed to recall your specific information on subsequent visits. This simplifies the process of recording your personal information, such as billing addresses, shipping addresses, and so on. When you return to the same Unleashed website, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use the Unleashed features that you customized.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the Unleashed services or websites you visit.

Security of your Personal Information

Unleashed secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. Unleashed secures the personally identifiable information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. Unleashed also keeps information in SAAS provided products.

Changes to this Statement
